(a)
Is \(\{{2x^{2}-1},{2x-4x^{2}},{4x^{3}-2x^{2}},{24x^{2}-8x-4}\}\) a basis for \(V\text{?}\)
(b)
What is the dimension of \(U+W\text{?}\)
(c)
What is the dimension of \(U\cap W\text{?}\)
