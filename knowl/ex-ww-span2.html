Exercise 2.

Let \(V\) be the vector space \(P_3(\mathbb{R}\) of polynomials in \(x\) with degree less than 3 and \(W\) be the subspace
\begin{equation*} W=\operatorname{span} \left\lbrace {5+3x+3x^{2}},{6x-5-2x^{2}} \right\rbrace . \end{equation*}
  1. Find a nonzero polynomial \(p(x)\) in \(W\text{.}\)
  2. Find a polynomial \(q(x)\) in \(V\) that does not belong to \(W\text{.}\)
