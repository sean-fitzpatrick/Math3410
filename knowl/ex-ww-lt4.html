Exercise 5.

Let \(V\) and \(W\) be vector spaces and let \(\vec{v}_1, \vec{v}_2 \in V\) and \(\vec{w}_1, \vec{w}_2 \in W\text{.}\)
  • Suppose \(T : V \to W\) is a linear transformation.
    Find \(T( 5 \vec{v}_1 - \vec{v}_2)\) and write your answer in terms of \(T(\vec{v}_1)\) and \(T(\vec{v}_2)\text{.}\)
  • Suppose \(L : V \to W\) is a linear transformation such that \(L(\vec{v}_1) = \vec{w}_1 + \vec{w}_2\) and \(L(\vec{v}_2) = - 8 \vec{w}_2\text{.}\)
    Find \(L(4 \vec{v}_1 + 7 \vec{v}_2)\) in terms of \(\vec{w}_1\) and \(\vec{w}_2\text{.}\)
