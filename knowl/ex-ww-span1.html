Exercise 1.

Let \(V\) be the vector space of symmetric \(2\times 2\) matrices and \(W\) be the subspace
\begin{equation*} W=\operatorname{span} \left\{ {\left[\begin{array}{cc} 2 \amp -5\cr -5 \amp -3 \end{array}\right]},{\left[\begin{array}{cc} 0 \amp 4\cr 4 \amp 4 \end{array}\right]} \right\} . \end{equation*}
  1. Find a nonzero element \(X\) in \(W\text{.}\)
  2. Find an element \(Y\) in \(V\) that is not in \(W\text{.}\)
