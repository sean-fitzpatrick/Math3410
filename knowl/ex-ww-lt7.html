\begin{equation*} T(\vec{v}_1)={\left[\begin{array}{c} 1\cr -17 \end{array}\right]} \ \mbox{ and } \ T(\vec{v}_2)={\left[\begin{array}{c} 5\cr -13 \end{array}\right]} . \end{equation*}
Find the image \(T \left({\left[\begin{array}{c} x\cr y\cr \end{array}\right]}\right)\) of an arbitrary vector \({\left[\begin{array}{c} x\cr y\cr \end{array}\right]} .\)
