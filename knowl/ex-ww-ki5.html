Exercise 6.

Let \(f : \mathbb{R}^{2} \to \mathbb{R}^{2}\) be the linear transformation defined by \(f(x,y) = \langle {5x+y}, {3x-3y} \rangle\text{.}\)
  1. Find the matrix of the linear transformation \(f\text{.}\)
  2. The linear transformation \(f\) is
    • injective
    • surjective
    • bijective
    • none of these
  3. If \(f\) is bijective, find the matrix of its inverse.
