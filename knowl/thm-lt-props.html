Theorem 2.1.2.

Let \(T:V\to W\) be a linear transformation. Then
  1. \(T(\zer_V) = \zer_W\text{,}\) and
  2. For any scalars \(c_1,\ldots, c_n\) and vectors \(\vv_1,\ldots, \vv_n\in V\text{,}\)
    \begin{equation*} T(c_1\vv_1+c_2\vv_2+\cdots + c_n\vv_n) = c_1T(\vv_1)+c_2T(\vv_2)+\cdots + c_nT(\vv_n)\text{.} \end{equation*}
