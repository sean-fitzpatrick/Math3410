Exercise 8.

Let \(P_2\) be the vector space of all polynomials of degree 2 or less, and let \(H\) be the subspace spanned by \({7x^{2}-10x-5}, \ {19x^{2}-7x-7}\) and \({-\left(3x+1\right)}\text{.}\)
(a) What is the dimension of the subspace \(H\text{?}\)
(b) Is \(\lbrace {7x^{2}-10x-5}, {19x^{2}-7x-7}, {-\left(3x+1\right)} \rbrace\) a basis for \(P_2\text{?}\)
Be sure you can explain and justify your answer.
(c) Give a basis for the subspace \(H\text{.}\)
