Let \(M_{n,n}(\mathbb{R})\) denote the vector space of \(n \times n\) matrices with real entries. Let \(f : M_{2,2}(\mathbb{R}) \to M_{2,2}(\mathbb{R})\) be the function defined by \(f(A) = A^T\) for any \(A \in M_{2,2}(\mathbb{R})\text{.}\) Is \(f\) a linear transformation?
Let \(A = {\left[\begin{array}{cc} a_{11} \amp a_{12}\cr a_{21} \amp a_{22}\cr \end{array}\right]}\) and \(B = {\left[\begin{array}{cc} b_{11} \amp b_{12}\cr b_{21} \amp b_{22}\cr \end{array}\right]}\) be any two matrices in \(M_{2,2}(\mathbb{R})\) and let \(c \in \mathbb{R}\text{.}\)
  • \(f(A+B) =\)
    \(f(A) + f(B) =\) \(+\) .
    Does \(f(A+B) = f(A) + f(B)\) for all \(A, B \in M_{2,2}(\mathbb{R})\text{?}\)
    • Yes, they are equal
    • No, they are not equal
  • \(f(c A) =\) .
    \(c(f(A)) =\) \(\Bigg(\) \(\Bigg)\text{.}\)
    Does \(f(c A) = c(f(A))\) for all \(c \in \mathbb{R}\) and all \(A \in M_{2,2}(\mathbb{R})\text{?}\)
    • Yes, they are equal
    • No, they are not equal
  • Is \(f\) a linear transformation?
    • f is a linear transformation
    • f is not a linear transformation
