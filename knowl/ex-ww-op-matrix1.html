Exercise 1.

Let \(\vec{b}_1 = {\left[\begin{array}{c} 1\cr -2 \end{array}\right]}\) and \(\vec{b}_2= {\left[\begin{array}{c} -3\cr 7 \end{array}\right]}.\) The set \(B= \left\lbrace \vec{b}_1, \vec{b}_2 \right\rbrace\) is a basis for \({\mathbb R}^2.\)
Let \(T: {\mathbb R}^2 \rightarrow {\mathbb R}^2\) be a linear operator such that \(T(\vec{b}_1) = 6 \vec{b}_1 + 2 \vec{b}_2\) and \(T(\vec{b}_2) = 8 \vec{b}_1 + 2 \vec{b}_2.\)
  1. Find the matrix \(M_B(T)\) of \(T\) relative to the basis \(B\text{.}\)
  2. Find the matrix \(M_E(T)\) of \(T\) relative to the standard basis \(E\) for \({\mathbb R}^2\text{.}\)
