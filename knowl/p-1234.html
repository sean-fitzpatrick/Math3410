As a more practical example, consider the following (over-simplified) predator-prey system. It is based on an example in Interactive Linear Algebra 1 , by Margalit, Rabinoff, and Williams, but adapted to the wildlife here in Lethbridge. An ecosystem contains both coyotes and deer. Initially, there is a population of \(20\) coyotes, and \(500\) deer.
