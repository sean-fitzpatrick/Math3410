Let \(T:V\to V\) be a linear operator. If the characteristic polynomial of \(T\) is given by

\begin{equation*} c_T(x) = (x-\lambda_1)^{m_1}(x-\lambda_2)^{m_2}\cdots (x-\lambda_k)^{m_k}\text{,} \end{equation*}

then \(\dim G_{\lambda_j}(T)=m_j\) for each \(j=1,\ldots, k\text{.}\)
