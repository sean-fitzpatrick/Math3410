Unsurprisingly, this property is inherited from the distributive property of the real numbers:
\begin{align*} k(\vv+\ww) \amp = k\langle a+c,b+d\rangle\\ \amp = \langle k(a+c),k(b+d)\rangle \\ \amp = \langle ka+kc,kb+kd\rangle\\ \amp = \langle ka, kb\rangle+\langle kc, kd\rangle\\ \amp k\langle a,b\rangle + k\langle c,d\rangle = k\vv+k\ww\text{.} \end{align*}
